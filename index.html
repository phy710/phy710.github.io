Hongyi Pan

I am now a Postdoc at Northwestern University, collaborating with Prof. Ulas Bagci, since June 2023. I received my Ph.D. degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering from University of Illinois Chicago in 2023, advised by Prof. Ahmet Enis Cetin.

My research interests include image processing, computer vision, and digital signal processing, with a focus on image classification, object detection, orthogonal-transform-based neural networks, and kernel PCA.
