Hongyi Pan (潘弘毅)

I am now a Postdoc at Northwestern University, collaborating with Prof. Ulas Bagci, since June 2023. I get my Ph.D degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering from University of Illinois Chicago in April 2023, working with Prof. Ahmet Enis Cetin.

My research interests include image processing, computer vision, and digital signal processing, with a focus on image classification, object detection, orthogonal-transform-based neural networks, and kernel PCA.
